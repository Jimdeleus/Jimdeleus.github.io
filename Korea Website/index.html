Explore the Top Spots in Korea

Discover the most beautiful places in Korea to visit and experience.

Gyeongbokgung Palace

Built in 1395, Gyeongbokgung Palace is the main royal palace of the Joseon dynasty. It's one of the most iconic sites in Seoul.

Jeju Island

Known for its natural beauty, Jeju Island offers stunning beaches, volcanic landscapes, and rich cultural experiences.

Bukchon Hanok Village

This traditional village in Seoul features hundreds of hanoks (traditional Korean houses) dating back to the Joseon Dynasty.

Namsan Seoul Tower

Offering panoramic views of Seoul, the Namsan Seoul Tower is a must-visit for breathtaking cityscapes, especially at night.
